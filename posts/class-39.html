Class 39 Guided Reading Questions

MLK Letter from a Birmingham Jail

show participation instructions

In what way does MLK echo what Socrates has been saying in the Gorgias?
Do you recognize the characters of Polus or Callicles in any of the critics or opponents that MLK addresses in his letter?
Socrates ends his speech with a description of the after-life. MLK was likewise a religious person who believed in an after-life. How important do you think this belief was for their ability to pursue the truth over flattery? Would they have been willing to make themselves vulnerable to injustice and ultimately die without this belief?
